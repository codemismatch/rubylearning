Tooling checklist

  • Version managers: rbenv or RVM to isolate Ruby installs.
  • Package management: Bundler for gems, RubyGems to discover libraries.
  • Quality: RuboCop for linting, RSpec or Minitest for testing.
  • Frameworks: Rails for full-stack apps, Hanami and Sinatra for alternative architectures.

Stay connected

  • Forums: discuss.rubyonrails.org for Rails guidance; request an invite to ruby.social Slack for daily chat.
  • Communities: r/ruby, r/rails, and the Ruby Discord.
  • News: Subscribe to Ruby Weekly for curated updates.
